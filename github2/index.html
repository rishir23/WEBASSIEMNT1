New Community Garden Brings Neighbors Together

GRAND OPENING CELEBRATION

Published: January 15, 2025 | Reading time: 3 minutes

The city's newest community garden officially opened last Saturday with a ribbon-cutting ceremony and free workshops for local families. Dozens of volunteers worked for months to transform an empty lot into a green space full of vegetables, flowers, and native plants.
