New Community Garden Brings Neighbors Together

Grand Opening Celebration

The city’s newest community garden officially opened last Saturday with a ribbon-cutting ceremony and free workshops for local families. Dozens of volunteers worked for months to transform an empty lot into a green space full of vegetables, flowers, and native plants.

According to the organizers, the garden will provide fresh produce for families and teach kids about healthy eating. “This project shows what we can do when we work together,” said volunteer coordinator Jamie Lee.

Next month, the garden team will host planting classes and garden tours for students. Anyone interested in volunteering can sign up at the community center.

“We want this garden to grow strong roots in our neighborhood.” — Jamie Lee

For more information, visit the official website or follow the garden’s updates on social media.
